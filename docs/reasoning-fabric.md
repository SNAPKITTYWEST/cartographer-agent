# Reasoning Fabric

The CARTO stack now has a higher layer above the kernels.

## Shape

- `CARTO / Prolog` is the auditor
- `COBOL` owns corporate chunks and institution-facing procedure
- `REXX` moves requests, summons, and workflow glue
- `Nemotron` is the synthesis and spawn engine
- `Gemini News` is the current-results and article engine
- `Tokio FSM` controls state transitions

## State Path

1. `attach_engine`
2. `audit_case`
3. `summon_kernel`
4. `spawn_meta_lawyer`
5. `seal_receipt`

## Meta-Lawyer Spawn Profiles

- `corporate_builder`
  - kernel base: `COBOL`
  - engine: `Nemotron`
  - output: formations, records packets, business filings

- `law_researcher`
  - kernel base: `CARTO / Prolog`
  - engine: `Gemini News`
  - output: legal terrain, current law results, article-backed reasoning

- `docket_runner`
  - kernel base: `REXX`
  - engine: `Nemotron`
  - output: summons routing, docket motion, repeated office procedure

## Summon Contract

REXX emits summon envelopes. CARTO must audit before spawn. Nemotron may only spawn a meta-lawyer after the attach and audit states succeed.
